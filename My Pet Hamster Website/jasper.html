All about Jasper <3

Basic information about Jasper

  • Age: 5 months
  • Gender: Male
  • Breed: Syrian
  • Color: White, black, and beige

Fun facts about Jasper

Jasper lived in Page House at Caltech! He enjoys the housing provided at Caltech, unlike many people.

His favorite hobby is running FAST and making tunnels in his home.

His favorite foods are oranges, bread, apples, and cereal. He also eats rice, chicken, pasta, and other human food.

While most hamsters sleep buried in their bedding, Jasper loves to sleep on his wheel with food in his mouth. He sleeps when everyone is in class and is very active at night.
